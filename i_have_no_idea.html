Types of Hackers

  1. White Hat Hackers
  2. Black Hat Hackers/Crakers
  3. Grey Hat Hackers

White Hat Hackers

White Hat Hackers are the good guys.They work for big companies on the security side.


Black Hat Hackers

These are the bad guys.They usually infect the victim with a malware,for example a virus,and wait something to exchange the healing with,usually money.


Grey Hat Hackers

These are the guys that are on both sides.They use their White Hat Hacker abilities in Black Hat Hackers activities.


Script-Kiddies

When someone uses prewritten scripts from real hackers to hack into computers, they are called script-kiddies.
